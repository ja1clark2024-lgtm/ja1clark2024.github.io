How it works

The routing layer sits between your platform and your acquirers. For each transaction, it evaluates real-time metrics— latency, cost, approval rate, geography—and selects the best acquirer. If an acquirer degrades or fails, traffic is automatically shifted to healthier routes.

1. Ingest

Your existing gateway or platform sends transactions to the routing layer via a simple API. No change to your checkout UX.

2. Evaluate

The patented match engine scores each acquirer using live and historical metrics: latency, MDR, approval rate, BIN, card type, and more.

3. Route & Learn

Transactions are routed to the optimal acquirer. Performance is continuously learned and updated, improving over time.
