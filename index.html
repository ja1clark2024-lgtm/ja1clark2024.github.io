How it works

The routing layer sits between your platform and your acquirers. For each transaction, it evaluates real-time metrics—latency, cost, approval rate, geography—and selects the best acquirer.

1. Ingest

Your existing gateway sends transactions to the routing layer via a simple API.

2. Evaluate

The patented match engine scores each acquirer using live and historical metrics.

3. Route & Learn

Transactions are routed to the optimal acquirer. Performance improves continuously.
